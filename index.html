STRICT COMPLIANCE BOARD

Hard Breach Framework

Violating any of the following operational parameters results in immediate account closure or profit adjustments. Trade with discipline.

Strategy Prohibitions

High-Frequency Trading (positions held under 60 seconds), automated Grid setups, One-Sided Bets lacking technical trend verification, and progressive Martingale volume-doubling on losing positions are strictly banned.

Data Execution Ban

Any exploitation of system pricing glitches, data feed latency arbitrage, reverse arbitrage, gap trading, toxic order flow manipulation, or tick scalping will void the account infrastructure instantly.

Risk Boundaries

Risking or losing >5% of the starting daily drawdown on a single idea is a hard breach. Single trade limits are capped at 1% to 3% of the initial balance. Positions closed and re-entered in the same asset within 10 minutes count as the same trade idea.

Account Integrity

External account management, group trade copying, public third-party EAs, and group hedging are completely forbidden. Day padding using artificial micro-lot sizes simply to meet minimal active day rules is banned. News trading positions cannot be opened or closed within a 4-minute window before or after high-impact economic releases.

Read Carefully: We Are Not An Overnight Wealth Scheme.

90% of retail speculators blow their accounts because of greed, overleveraging, and lack of internal risk controls. We do not provide automated shortcuts, magical signals, or effortless wealth. If you cannot or will not trade within the strict drawdown and compliance parameters defined above, do not purchase this package. We only cultivate disciplined professionals.
